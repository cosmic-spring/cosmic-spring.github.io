Earendel

The most distant star known

seen as it was 13 billion years ago

Earendel is a star in a distant galaxy known as the Sunrise Arc, which is gravitationally lensed and magnifed by a galaxy cluster WHL0137-08.

Brian Welch, who discovered Earendel with Hubble, presents an analysis of new images obtained with JWST NIRCam in 8 filters spanning 0.8 – 5.0 µm: F090W, F115W, F150W, F200W, F277W, F356W, F410M, F444W.
