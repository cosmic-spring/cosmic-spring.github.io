Welcome to Cosmic Spring!

We are astronomers sharing beautiful images and science results from James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) observations of distant galaxies in the early universe magnified by gravitational lensing. The combined powers of JWST + lensing are revealing tiny details we've never seen before!

Projects

JWST observations of gravitational lensing

Below are just a few JWST programs observing gravitational lensing by galaxy clusters. Others include GLASS, TEMPLATES, UNCOVER, CANUCS, and PEARLS.

WHL0137 + Sunrise Arc + Earendel

A galaxy cluster magnifying a distant galaxy and an individual star / star system, observed by JWST in July with more planned for December.

MACS0647-JD

One of the most distant galaxies ever discovered with Hubble, to be observed by JWST this fall!

Webb's First Deep Field

SMACS 0723 was the first galaxy cluster observed by JWST and the inspiration for Cosmic Spring.
