Team

A Team Effort

The James Webb Space Telescope was developed, engineered, and launched by 20,000 people. We are eternally grateful for their efforts devlivering such an incredible discovery machine!

We are a group of astronomers helping to make these discoveries and sharing the science and pretty pictures with the world. Many of us are also working on other science programs; we contribute here when we can.
