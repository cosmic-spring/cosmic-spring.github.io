Welcome to Cosmic Spring!

We are astronomers sharing beautiful images and science results from the James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) observations of distant galaxies in the early universe magnified by gravitational lensing. The combined powers of JWST + lensing are revealing tiny details we've never seen before!

Earendel JWST paper

Brian Welch discovered Earendel with Hubble and has now led a analysis of new images from JWST.
